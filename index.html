My favorite books

Tools of Titans

Tim Ferris

This is my favorite book because I think it is such a huge source of inspiration, motivation, this is basically the bible for me. When I struggle to achieve what I want to do, I take 15 minutes for my self and read this book. It is basically a compilation of very small chapter, 3 to 5 pages, relating the tips of high achiever more or less known people whether they are politicians, athelts or artists.

Start With Why

Simon Sinek

This is my second favorite book :) But I have to admit that it comes ver close with the first one! This one is less about achievements than about self developement and knowing what you really like doing deep down. It is a bit about finding your life purpose. It is not a big book compared to The Tools of Titans! It tells you about how passion makes you overcome any challenge life throuws at you
